# Quality Rubric (LinkedIn)

Score the draft before delivery. Anything that fails gets fixed, not shipped. Ten checks, pass/fail. If more than one fails, revise and re-score.

## The checks

1. **One idea.** The post makes a single, clear argument. If a reader can't say what it was about in one sentence, cut until they can.

2. **Hook earns the click.** First line is under ~210 characters, specific, and creates a reason to expand "…see more." It's honest — the body delivers what the hook implies.

3. **Every factual claim is sourced.** Each stat, study, quote, and date maps to a row in the research source log. No "studies show" without a named study. No invented numbers.

4. **Recency and primacy.** Load-bearing facts are current and, where possible, from primary sources. Dates checked.

5. **Skimmable.** Short paragraphs, hard line breaks, white space. No walls of text. Reads in 60-90 seconds.

6. **Concrete takeaway.** The reader leaves with one specific, usable thing — not a platitude. It passes the "so what do I do Monday?" test.

7. **Zero-click.** ~80% of the value is in the post. No outbound link in the body; any link is routed to the first comment.

8. **Real close, no bait.** Ends with a genuine question or invitation. No "comment YES," no "tag 3 people," no like-begging.

9. **Voice and cleanliness.** Matches any specified voice/config. No prohibited words. No AI tells (this is enforced hard by the required ai-slop-killer pass, but check here too).

10. **Length in range.** ~1,300-1,900 characters unless the format deliberately calls for shorter/longer, and every extra line earns its place.

## Saveability bonus (aim for it, not required)

- Would a reader **save** this to reference later? Frameworks, checklists, specific how-tos, and curated lists earn saves — the strongest 2026 signal. If the post is purely reactive/opinion, that's fine, but a saveable post outperforms.

## Final gate

- [ ] Rubric: all 10 pass (or the failures are deliberate and noted).
- [ ] Source log complete: every claim → URL.
- [ ] **ai-slop-killer has run on the post copy** and reported clean (or its fixes applied). Non-negotiable.

Report the score and the ai-slop-killer confirmation in the delivery.
